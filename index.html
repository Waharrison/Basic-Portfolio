About Me

William Harrison was born March 11, 1985 in Greensboro, NC. Graduated from UNC-Greensboro in 2007 with a bachelor degree in Small Business. After college William went to work for his family business at Graham Sporting Goods in Burlington, NC. After 5 years he moved back to Greensboro and started the website GrahamSG.com.
